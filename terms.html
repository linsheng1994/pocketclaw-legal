1. Service Description

PocketClaw is the official iOS companion app for the OpenClaw framework, providing remote management capabilities for OpenClaw instances running on your personal device. Both PocketClaw and OpenClaw are developed and maintained by the same developer. This app requires OpenClaw Gateway and the pocketclaw-server plugin to be installed on your personal computer. PocketClaw itself does not serve as a public generative service provider. All capabilities are provided by user-configured local instances.

2. Usage Conditions

To use PocketClaw, you need: • A personal computer running OpenClaw • The pocketclaw-server plugin installed and Gateway running • Device pairing completed via QR code or manual setup You confirm that you will only use this app on devices you are authorized to manage.
